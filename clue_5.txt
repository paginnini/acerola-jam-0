church of the second son public behavior rules

1- Red meat in the winter is not to be eaten;

2- When in presence of a first son member, always stay right behind or next to him when in a line or equivalent, everything you do is after the first son does, sit, serve, eat,come and go,
if you cannot hold yourself as not the center of the Son's attention, do not expect to be a son on the home of tomorrow;

3- Never speak ill of the church or a member to an outsider, only if you wish to become one;

4- Never cry in public;

5- Public restrooms may only be used in emergencies, and when used confess to the clergyman or assistant to be purified;

6- Socializing with maltzecloth is punishable with whipping for purification;

7- Do not speak in the name of the Son;
